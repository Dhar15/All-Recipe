  • 3 Tbsp oil

  • Chopped coriander leaves to taste

    • {%endblock%} {% block recipe %}
    1. Chop jackfruit into tiny pieces.
    2. Marinate the chicken drumsticks with turmeric, ginger garlic paste, green chilli paste, salt and lemon juice. In a kadai heat oil and add cumin seeds.
    3. Now boil them in water and keep aside.

      4. For the main preparation:

    4. Heat oil in pan. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, curry leaves, garlic paste, chopped onions, turmeric powder, crushed red chillies, chopped tomatoes salt and tamarind water.
    5. Saute till the onions turn light brown. Now add the prepared jackfruit, chopped coriander leaves, cashew nuts and garam masala. Mix it well.
    6. Panas Puttu Koora is ready to eat. Serve hot with rice.
