  • 500 Gram Lamb curry (cut)
  • 200 Gram Coconut milk powder
  • 2 packet Bhuna masala
  • 1 Tbsp Coconut oil
  • 1 tsp Black mustard seeds
  • Few Dry red chillies
  • 1 tsp Urad dal
  • 1 Tbsp Curry leaves
  • 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  • To taste Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Star anise
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 Fresh coconut, grated
  • 1 Tbsp Tamarind paste (or tamarind soaked in water)
  • 1 tsp Jaggery (gud)
  • Few springs Fresh coriander
    • {% endblock %} {% block recipe %}
    1. Clean lamb. Keep it dry.
    2. Place lamb in pressure cooker along with coconut milk, one packet bhuna masala and cook till lamb is tender. Keep aside.
    3. Heat coconut oil in a pan.
    4. Add black mustard seed, red chilli, ural dal and curry leaf.
    5. Also add garam masala powder, black pepper powder, salt and whole spices like star anise and cinnamon, and sauté.
    6. Add grated coconut, sauté well.
    7. Add bhuna masala. Sauté well till it leaves the side of the pan.
    8. Add tamarind paste and jaggery. Mix well.
    9. Add coconut milk and simmer till it blends. Do not over boil.
    10. Add chopped coriander leaves.
    11. Now add cooked lamb in the coconut curry and simmer for a few minutes.
    12. Serve hot. Garnish with fried coconut chips, dry red chilli and coriander sprigs.
